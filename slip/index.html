FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can I use it when the internet is down?

Yes. Slip is local-first, so checkout and records keep working offline.

Is Slip POS an offline POS app for iPad?

Yes. Slip POS runs on iPad and is built for teams that need offline checkout, receipt printing, and reliable cash reconciliation on iOS.

What happens after the print cap is reached?

Your data stays safe and completed records remain intact; you can choose to unlock printing.

Will my data disappear after I cancel?

No. Your data remains on your device.

What kinds of shops is Slip POS for?

Slip POS is built for cafes, small shops, market stalls, and food service teams that need an offline iPad POS with reliable checkout, receipt printing, and cash reconciliation.
