Privacy Policy

Slip POS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

All data entered into the app — including sales records, settings, and menu items — is stored locally on the user's device. Slip POS operates entirely offline and does not use any network services, analytics tools, or cloud storage.

We do not track user behavior, collect location data, or access personal information in any form. If any network-based features (such as cloud backup or online syncing) are introduced in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
