First 10 Operators Program

Slip First 10 Operators Program

Slip is looking for the first 10 shops to use Slip in real operations.

Purpose

This is not a promo campaign or an endorsement request.

We want to learn what Slip gets right -- and what it doesn't -- in the real world.

What you'll get

  • After acceptance, we'll provide 3 or 6 months of subscription discount codes
  • Full printing features
  • Priority access to fixes and improvements
  • After the discount period, you choose whether to continue

What we hope you'll do (fully optional)

If you're willing to share your real experience after some time (good or bad):

  • A short written note or a few quick answers
  • No public posting required
  • It won't affect your ability to keep using Slip

Important notes

  • We will not ask for App Store reviews
  • We will not ask for public endorsements
  • Your data always stays on your device
