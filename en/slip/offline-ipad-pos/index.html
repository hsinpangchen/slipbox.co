An offline POS app for iPad, built for small shops that cannot stop the line.

What this page is really about

If you're searching for an offline iPad POS, you're usually not looking for a huge all-in-one retail suite. You're looking for a POS app that keeps taking orders, printing receipts, and recording sales even when the network is unreliable.

Slip POS is built for exactly that kind of floor reality on iPad and iOS.

Why offline matters for an iPad POS

  • Your line should not stop because the shop Wi-Fi drops.
  • A market stall or pop-up may not have stable internet at all.
  • At close, you still need records and cash reconciliation to make sense.

Best fit for

  • Small shops that want a simpler iPad POS
  • Cafes and food counters that need fast receipt printing
  • Market stalls and temporary booths with unstable internet
  • Operators who care more about reliable floor flow than cloud-heavy admin

Built around iPad and iOS use on the floor

Slip POS runs on iPad and is designed around real on-site usage: quick checkout, printer connection, and end-of-day cash handling.

It is not trying to make you depend on an account before you can sell. The goal is to keep the POS usable on the device in front of you.

What makes Slip different

Many POS tools talk about features first. Slip starts with three things a small operator actually notices when service gets busy: whether checkout still works offline, whether receipts print when they should, and whether the cash closes cleanly.

That makes Slip a better match for people specifically searching for an offline POS app for iPad, not a generic POS platform.
