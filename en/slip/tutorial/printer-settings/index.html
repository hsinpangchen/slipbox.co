Printer setup & testing (beginner)

If this is your first printer setup, learn the layout first, then pair real hardware.

This flow starts from Printer management and walks through routing and template preview.

Prerequisites:

  • You are currently on iPad Settings
  • You have entered the settings screen with the sidebar

Steps

  1. Step 1 (F01): Open the Printer screen

    Tap Printer in the left sidebar and confirm you're in the correct module.

    • Register button appears at top-right
    • Registered printer list appears in the center
    • Receipt template section appears below

  2. Step 2 (F02): Start printer registration

    Tap Register at top-right to enter brand selection.

    • You can see Star / Epson brand cards
    • Next is initially disabled

  3. Step 3 (F03): Choose brand and go to pairing guide

    Select a brand (for example, Star), then tap Next.

    • You enter the iOS Bluetooth pairing instructions
    • You can see I have paired

  4. Step 4 (F04): Go to device discovery

    On the pairing guide screen, tap I have paired.

    • You enter the discovery step
    • You can see Retry discovery

  5. Step 5 (F05): Check registered printer info

    Back on Printer management, tap a registered printer.

    • Printer info screen opens
    • Default Drawer toggle is visible

  6. Step 6 (F06): Open receipt template routing

    In Receipt template, tap Checkout Receipt.

    • Print Routing screen opens
    • You can see printers that can be added to routing

  7. Step 7 (F07): Add one route

    On the routing screen, tap Add on the right side of a printer row.

    • The new route appears in the output order area

  8. Step 8 (F08): Open template preview

    Back on Printer management, tap the preview icon on the right of Checkout Receipt.

    • A template preview sheet opens
    • You can scroll and review the full receipt content

Common issues

  • Can't find registered printers: confirm registration completed, or re-open Printer management.
  • Device not detected: go to iOS Settings > Bluetooth and confirm printer is paired and powered on.
